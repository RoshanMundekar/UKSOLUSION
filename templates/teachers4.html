General Practitioner (GP) Registration

Locate a Local GP Clinic

1.Research nearby GP clinics using online directories or recommendations.

2.Choose a clinic that is convenient for you in terms of location and operating hours.

STEP-1

Register with a GP

1.Contact the chosen GP clinic to inquire about their registration process.

2.Schedule an appointment for registration, if required..

3.Complete the necessary registration forms provided by the clinic.

STEP-2

Provide Necessary Identification and Proof of Address

1.Bring valid identification documents such as passport, national ID, or driving license.

2.Provide proof of address, which can include a utility bill or a bank statement with your current address.

3.If you're an international student, also bring documentation related to your visa status.

STEP-3

Attend the Registration Appointment:

1.Attend the scheduled registration appointment at the GP clinic.

2.Submit the completed registration forms along with your identification and proof of address.

STEP-4

Receive Confirmation and Access Services

1.Once registered, you should receive confirmation of your registration.

2.You can then access healthcare services provided by the GP clinic.

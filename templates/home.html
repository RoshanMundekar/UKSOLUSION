Arrival in the UK

BR Collection

If by "BR," you mean Biometric Residence Permit (BRP), you should collect it within 10 days of your arrival. This is usually at the post office mentioned in your visa application

Bank Account

Open a bank account for your stay. Visit a local bank branch with the required identification documents, proof of address, and your BRP.

National Insurance Number (Ni Number)

Apply for a National Insurance Number if you plan to work in the UK. You can usually do this by calling the National Insurance number application line

Register with GP (General Practitioner)

Find a local GP (doctor) and register as soon as possible. You'll need proof of address and your BRP. This ensures you have access to healthcare services.

Bank Account

Research and Choose a Suitable Bank

1.Explore different banks and compare their student account offerings.

2.Consider factors such as fees, overdraft options, online services, and branch accessibility.

STEP-1

Visit the Bank in Person with Necessary Identification Documents

1.Passport or national ID.

2.Proof of address (e.g., utility bill or rental agreement)

3.Letter of acceptance from your educational institution

4.Confirmation of student status (if applicable)

STEP-2

Open a Student Bank Account

1.Schedule an appointment or visit the bank during their operating hours.

2.Present your identification documents to the bank representative.

3.Complete the necessary application forms provided by the bank.

4.Provide any additional information required, such as your course details and expected graduation date.

5.Discuss and choose any optional features, like overdraft facilities.

6.Receive your new student bank account details, including account number and sort code.
